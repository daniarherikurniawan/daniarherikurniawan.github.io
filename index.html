Hi, my name is Daniar, but you can call me Dan. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Chicago, where I am a member of the UCARE research group and have the privilege of being advised by Professor Haryadi Gunawi. I received my bachelor degree from Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB), Indonesia, where I graduated with a cum laude distinction.

I have a strong background in Machine Learning and Distributed Systems, with over 7 years of experience in Cloud Systems research. I enjoy tackling difficult research questions and managing projects from start to finish. In addition to my own research, I have also mentored over 10 undergraduate and master's students in a research mentorship program over the past three years. In total, I have published nine papers in peer-reviewed conferences, with four of them being first-author contributions. Some of these papers have been accepted at top Systems Conferences such as ASPLOS, OSDI, SIGCOMM, and EUROSYS
